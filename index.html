About Me

Web Developer and Internet Marketing Strategist

I develop websites & internet marketing strategies, implement organic and paid search campaigns, monitor campaigns, and use detailed quantitative analysis to always continue growing the ROI, traffic, and presence of my clients' businesses. I have particular specialities in front-end web development and UX design, WordPress theme development, & LAMP server administration. In addition, I am very capable in all aspects of search marketing (both paid and organic), including writing content that both emphasizes user experience and search visibility.

I am of the mindset that digital marketing should always integrate the discipline of user experience (UX) design across all channels, and as such I always build websites and campaigns with the user in mind. Internet marketing may be divided into several different types of work but I believe a strong understanding, along with experience, in the implementation of the many parts benefits the client more than a silo approach. I see my role as a digital marketing expert as one of keeping all the moving parts working together and understanding how each one works individually. I make it my personal goal to understand the overall goal of a client which, in combination with comprehensive knowledge & experience, allows me to work more cohesively and efficiently with the different resources focused on that shared goal.
